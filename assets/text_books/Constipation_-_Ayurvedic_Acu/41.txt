The main food groups and their recommended
proportions within a balanced diet.

This pyramidal chart is based on Balanced diet
which includes high number of calories with other
nutritional value from top to bottom. Where we
see that bread, rice, cereals and pasta (low calories
and higher nutritional value) has been given largest
area of the pyramidal chart and fat, oil and sweets
(high calories and low nutritional value) given a
little one place. Thus fat, oil and sweets should be
use sparingly if at all.

Certain groups of individuals may require a
diet different from the principles outlined above.
