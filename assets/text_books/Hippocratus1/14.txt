12) Peptic Ulcer :

Sores in membrane of stomach and duodenum is called peptic ulcer.
In general gas and burning 2-3 hours after meals are the general symptoms.

Usually pain appears in chest region only but it is observed in dorsal (back)
side too when sores are in dorsal wall of duodenum.
