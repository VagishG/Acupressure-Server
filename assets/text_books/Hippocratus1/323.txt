39) Kidney Failure :

When Kidney function is deficient and does not perform its efficiency, such
condition is called Kidney or Renal failure.

1) Semi coma/Coma

2) Hypertension

3) Poor Metabolism

The above causes :

a) Increase of waste products of protein metabolisms viz. urea, uric-acid,
creatinine, etc in blood.

b) Imbalance of water & ions in body.
3) Acidic & basic (pH) imbalance in body.
Uremia :

Increased urea in blood is the basic cause of renal failure; urea is formed in Liver
by ammonia and Carbon dioxide because urea is soluble in water therefore it is excreted
in urine through kidney.
