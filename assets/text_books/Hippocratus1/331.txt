61) Urethritis :

This is more common in women, because space between anus & vulva is 1-1/:
Therefore E. Coli. easily enters in vulva and infects vagina & urethra.

Urethra in men gets infected by bacteria called Gogocons.
Symptoms :

1) Dysuria (unbearable pain & burning while urination)

2) Dribbling of urine

3) Purulent discharge: urination followed by fluids or pus.
4) Hesitation to urinate.
