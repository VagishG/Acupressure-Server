27) Whooping Cough :

This infection usually infects children which preliminary looks simple cough.
Minor attacks are of whoop that stops with loud exhalation and sound. Child's face and
eyes look red, lips blue and tongue comes out. Vomiting with phlegm may be companied.
After whoop child is lethargic.
