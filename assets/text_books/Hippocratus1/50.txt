30) Cellulitis :

Attack of Streptococcal bacteria on connective tissues (situated below skin),
obstructs lymph vessels therefore a wide area of skin is affected. Slight redness and
swelling is observed on skin but comparatively disease is more painful. Ignoring this
will encourage the above limits via swelling in lymph node/vessels. Regional lymph
node size may increase and they too will be painful.
