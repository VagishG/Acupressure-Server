10) Chikun Gunya:

Chikun Gunya usually starts suddenly with fever, chills, headache, nausea,
vomiting, joint pain and rashes. In Swahili language(an A frican Language) ‘Chikun Gunya’

means “that which bends up”. This refers to the stooped posture of patients who are
afflicted with severe joint pain. While recovery from Chikun Gunya is the expected
out come rarely joint pain may be prolonged up to a year or more. Infection appears to
