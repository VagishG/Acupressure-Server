7) Laryngitis :
Larynx is preceeded by naso-pharynx and followed by trachea.

Vocal cords are a part of it. Air passing through vocal cords, help to create sound/
voice. Infection in larynx is observed as change in voice. Sore throat, whooping and
slight fever are the general symptoms.
