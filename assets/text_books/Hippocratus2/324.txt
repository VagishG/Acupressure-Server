10) Tracheitis :

Inflammation in trachea arrives from tonsils, pharynx and larynx or if lungs are
infected. Lumen of trachea decreases due to inflammation.

Symptoms: Difficulty in inspiration and soreness behind sternum bone. No prob-
lem in exhalation but voice will be changed.
