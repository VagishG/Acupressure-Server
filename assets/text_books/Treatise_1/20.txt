Four Needle

In Deficiency conditions :

a) Select more local points.

b) Apply tonification technique to these local points.

c) Usemoxa or warming technique, wherever need be.

d) Useless distal points.

e) Apply neutral technique to these distal points.

Rational :

i) Local points are the main points to tonify deficient energetic function.

i) Distal points are used in deficiency condition to harmonize the local tonifying
action.
