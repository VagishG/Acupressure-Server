Q Vata Eyes are small and nervous with drooping eyelids and dry, scanty lashes.
The white of the eye is muddy, while the iris is dark, grey-black

Q Pitta Eyes are moderate in size, sharp, lustrous and sensitive to light. The lashes
are scanty and oily while the iris is red or yellowish.

Q Kapha Eyes are large, beautyful and moist with long thick oily lashes. The white

of the eye is very white while the iris is pale, blue or black.
