Acute myocardial infarction are of three types according to Chinese Acupuncture :
1) Acute & chronic heart yang deficiency

2) Obstruction in heart caused by stagnated phlegm
3) Heartyang collapse

Acute & chronic heart yang deficiency :
Pathogenesis :

i) Gradual decline of heart Qi

ii) Chronic heart disease

ii) Obstruction in heart due to stagnation of phlegm
iv) Damage of body of heart

v) _ Asthenia of fundamental : Deficiency of Qi, blood, yin and yang.
vi) Asthenia of the incidental

vii) Weak anal opening

Main Symptoms :

During attack of disease :

e@ Sudden fainting & collapse

e Profuse sweating

e@ = Cold limbs

e  Pallorface

After recovery of conciousness :

@ —Oppressive chest pain

@ — Listlessness

e Pale tongue

e = Thready & indistinct pulse, knotted or intermittent
Treatment Principles :

i) Toraise the deficient yang

ii) Totreat cold limbs

ili) To astringe the yin fluids

iv) Torescue collapse
