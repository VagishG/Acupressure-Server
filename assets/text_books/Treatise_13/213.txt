TCM protocols for treatment of kidney disease

Causes of diseases

(i) Prolonged illness

(ii) Weak constitution

(iii) Over indulgence in sex

(iv) Overwork over a long period

(v) Depletion of body fluids due to fever

(vi) Loss of blood due to any reason

(vii) After taking sex stimulating medicines

Due to deficiency of kidney yin, production of marrow reduces and the patient
becomes weak, he feels giddy and in the ears, sounds of flowing water start
coming.

Due to kidney yin deficiency, increase of empty heat causes 5 heart heat to
rise and night sweating takes place, tongue becomes red and pulse rate
increases. Due to increase in empty heat, night sweating causes nutritious
elements to flow away with the sweat.
