It is a fleshy growth of the conjunctiva that spreads across the cornea.

Canthus outcrop creeping over the eye.

Cause of disease :

1) Excess wind heat in liver causes initially red eyes and in the long run causes
ulcers of eyes.

2) Excess heart heat together with wind heat in liver in the long run causes
pterygium.

Symptoms:

1) Itching or pain, a fleshy, sinusy, membraneous, excrescence in both canthi.

2) Continuous anxiety & worry, if there is excess heart heat.

3) A fleshy outcropping attacks the eyes.
