Treatment of dry eyes :
i) Mild cases of dry eyes can be treated with eye drop that mimic the natural tear
film.
ii) Stopping of smoking provides considerable relief.
iti) Avoid direct wind, air conditioning & hair dryers.
iv) Use humidifier
v) Conciously blinking repeatedly helps spread tears more evenly.
