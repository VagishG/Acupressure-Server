Whatis Metabolism ?

Food and drinks we take are complex materials.

The body breaks the food and drinks into simpler substances.

This process involves several steps.

The simpler substances are then used to sustain life.

These simpler substances are carbohydrates, amino-acids, fats (lipids) and
proteins.

The process of breaking down and converting the substances ingested is called
metabolism.

How the metabolism is carried out ?

Metabolism is carried out by chemical substances called enzymes.
Enzymes are made by the body.
