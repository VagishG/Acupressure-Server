Depressed anger may cause liver Qi stagnation.

Stagnated liver Qi then attacks spleen & stomach.

Stagnated liver Qi causes distress in chest, hypochondriac pain and abdomi-
nal distension.

When the stagnated liver Qi attacks stomach, the stomach Qi fails to descend.
It causes belching and anorexia.

When the stagnated liver Qi attacks spleen. There is abdominal pain, vomiting
and abnormal bowel movement.

Distress in chest and all other symptoms mentioned above cause melancholia
and mental depression. This comes under the category of acute melancholia

referred to in (1) above.
