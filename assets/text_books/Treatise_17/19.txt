The pathogenesis of fever (sax @T SIgHia Ud fear) varies and is complicated,
more so, after the use of many antibiotics.

In such cases the principles of TCM syndrome differentiation diagnosis and
treatment given in this chapter may help combat the fever.

If the fever is due to Qi deficiency, replenish the vital energy and the fever will go
away.

If the fever is due to yin & essence deficiency, replenish yin and the fever will go
away.

If there is fever due to excess of yang, sedate yang and the fever will go away.
While treating fever of unknown origin, we have to differentiate between the
various causative factors, whether there is external attack or internal damage.
We have to study the nature of fever, type of fever, the pathogenesis of fever
etc.
