Heat exhaustion :

Heat exhaustion is excessive loss of salts (electrolytes) and fluids due to heat.
It causes decreased blood volume. Decreased blood volume causes fainting,
collapse or exhaution. Heat exhaustion is more severe than heat cramps.
Fluids & salts are more depleted and symptoms are more severe.
