Tripple Warmer Muscles Meridian

This meridian passes through, below of jaw to outer brow of eyes and goes to temple,
so treat this meridian for swelling of jaw and nodes or mumps.

The uses of meridian:
OG Spasm of tongue.
OSpasm and stiffness in the the pathway of this meridian.
