Radio ulna joint sprain is caused by a severe wrist injury, when the ligaments holding
the lower ends of the two wrist bones are stretched.

This causes radio ulna joint sprain. This causes weakness and pain on rotating the
wrist. There is pain in the wrist on rotating the round knob of a radio by hand due to ulna joint

sprain.
