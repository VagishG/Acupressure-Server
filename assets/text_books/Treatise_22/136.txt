Athlete's Foot saad va

Athlete's foot is the most common form of tinea.

Itis spread by direct contact, which commonly occurs when bare feet come in contact
with moist areas, such as changing rooms, bathrooms and pool area.

Main Symptoms :

Redness, swelling, scaling, cracking, burning and intense itching between the toes.
The skin may look white and puckered.

Many people with athlete's foot also have toe nail fungus, infection of the nails.
