Burning Feet

Acontinual burning or stinging sensation in the feet common over age 65. The condition
may be caused by :

i) Nerve damage called polyneuropathy

ii) Athletes foot

ili) Allergic reaction to shocks

iv) Chemicals in shoes

Following home remedies give relief :

i) Place the feet in cool water twice a day for 15 minutes.

ii) Reduce the amount of time you stand.

iii) Wear the shoes that do not cramt your feet.

iv) Wear socks made of cotton.
