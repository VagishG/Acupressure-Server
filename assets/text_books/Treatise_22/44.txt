Symptoms :

e Muscle spasms caused by a low calcium level may be the first sign of rickets in
infants.

e Older infants may be slow to sit and crawl. The space between the skull bones
(fontanelles) may be slow to close.

e — Inchildren aged 1 to 4 years bone growth may be abnormal, causing an abnormal
curve in the spine and bowleg or knock knees.

e These children may be slow to walk.

e — Forolder children and adolescents, walking is painful.

e — The pelvic bones may flatten, narrowing the birth canals in adolescent girls.

e — Inadults the bones, particularly the spine, pelvis and leg weaken.

e Affected areas may be painful to touch and fracture may occur.

Diagnosis :

The diagnosis of rickets and osteomalacia is based on symptoms and appearance of
bones on X-ray and a low level of Vitamin D byproducts in the blood.

Treatment :

i) Treatment involves taking daily vitamin D and calcium supplements by mouth.

ii) | People with a chronic liver or kidney disorder may require special formulation of
vitamin D supplements.
