Jet Lag

Sleep and awake disorder may occur in people whose sleep patterns have
been disrupted. They fall asleep at inappropriate times and then cannot sleep when they

should.

These sleep awake reversals often result from jet lag (especially when travelling from
east to west, working irregular night shifts) frequent changes in working hours.

Main Symptoms :

Irritability

Fatigue during the day

Problem in concentrating

Performance under stress

Sleep awake schedule disorder

Treatment: St 36, Li11, Tw3, 4, GV 26 Tall

Use these points during the flight and one to two days thereafter.
