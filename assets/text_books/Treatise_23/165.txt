and kidney yang.
iv) Deep & weak pulse also indicates spleen & kidney yang deficiency.
v) Headache are due to stomach Qi deficiency.
vi) Loose stools & abdominal pain are due to retention of humid cold in interior.
Treatment :
1) Spleen & kidney yang deficiency causing retention of humid cold.
K 3, 7, UB 20, 21, 23, 25, GV 4, St 25, 36, 37, CV6 (L&R) fall
2) Heart yang deficiency to take care of stasis of blood due to retention of cold.
P 4,6, 9,H7, 9, GV 11, UB 15 (L&R) fall

in total. The patient was advised to continue the same treatment twice a week for three
months to avoid recurrence.
