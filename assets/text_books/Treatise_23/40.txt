Case History No. 80

Acase of Spleen and Kidney Yang deficiency causing pain in lower back and
loose stools solved.

Patient : A man aged 25 years.

Main Symptoms Wea cert :

e — Chronic pain in the lower back

e Frequent and yellow urination

e Tiredness

e — Chilliness

e Loose stools

e Pale and wet tongue

e Weak pulse

Diagnosis :

The above symptoms indicate spleen yang deficiency and kidney yang deficiency both
chronic pain in the lower back and frequent and yellow urination indicate kidney yang
deficiency.

Loose stools are due to spleen yang deficiency. Tiredness, chilliness, pale and wet
tongue, weak pulse all indicate deficiency of yang Qi.

This is always a chronic condition. It represents a stage next to spleen yang deficiency,
when both spleen yang and kidney yang are deficient. Here, spleen yang deficiency is the
primary cause of disease.

When spleen yang is deficient, it can not transport nourishments to the limbs, which
feel cold.

Edema, feeling of cold in back and legs is due to deficiency of kidney yang.

Deficient kidney yang fails to transform the fluids in the abdomen, results in chronic
diarrhea.

i)  Totonify Kidney yang :

K 3, 7, UB 23, GV4, CV6(L&R) fall

ii) To tonify Spleen yang :

UB 20, 21, St 36, 37, 25, UB 25 (L&R) Tall
