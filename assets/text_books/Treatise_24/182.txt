35) Humid heat in stomach causes dull epigastric pain with nausea and vomiting
Main Symptoms ea wart :
Dull epigastric pain
Nausea
Vomiting
Oppression in chest
Dry mouth
Does not like to drink
Bitter taste in mouth
Asticky mouth
Red tongue with a sticky yellow coating
Rapid and slippery pulse
Aetiology:
At first there is spleen Qi deficiency. The spleen Qi is unable to transform and transport
food and fluid. This leads to humid heat.
With humid heat in stomach, there is also humid heat in spleen.
Treatment Principles :
i) Refrigerate heat
ii) Resolve humidity
iii) Normal the stomach
Treatment: Sp 6, 9, St8, 21, Li4, CV 10, 9, UB 21/ all

CV 12, UB 20T

Discussion :

Sp 6, 9, St 21 These three points expel humid heat from the stomach.

St 8, Li4 These two points resolve humidity from the head and cures
the frontal headache.

CV 10, 9, UB 21 These three points descend the stomach Qi and promote
the transformations of food.

CV 12, UB 20 : These two points tone spleen which in turn resolve humidity.

Special Note : Avoid eating fried foods, bananas and peanut.
