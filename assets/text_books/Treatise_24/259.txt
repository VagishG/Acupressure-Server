Dizziness includes the following sensation :

e — Light headedness

e Loss of balance in which the person feels insteady and about to fall.
e — Sense of spinning

e Avague spaced out feeling

e = Vertigo

e@ The person feels about to loose conciousness.

Dizziness can be due to four factors viz, wind, fire, phlegm and deficiency.

Distinguish the various types of dizziness by interrogation about the accompanying
symptoms.
