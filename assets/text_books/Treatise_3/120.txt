Whatis fibroid Degeneration?

Fibroud or fibroma is an overgrowth of connective tissue present in all body structures
between two cells.

Whatis Interstitial Degeneration?

\nterstitial means a space or gap in a tissue or organ. This tissue may remain vacant
or be filled with a tissue other than the tissue itself or the organ tissue. This is called
interstitial degeneration.
