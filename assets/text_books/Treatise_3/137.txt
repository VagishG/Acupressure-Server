There are three parts of eye :

1) Frontal part of the eye Sclera and cornea are there in this part of the eye.

2) Middle part of the eye Choroid, ciliary body and iris are there in this part
of eye.

3) Inner part of the eye In this part Retina is there.

First of all we will discuss Eye Orbit Meridian discovered by Dr. Voll. Eye orbit meridian
is located around the orbits of both the eyes. It has 14 points in total. They are shown as
OR 1 to OR 14 serial no.
