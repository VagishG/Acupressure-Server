ND Meridian

Bi syndromes come in upper motor neuron disease.
Flaccid syndromes come in lower motor neuron disease.

In Bi syndromes treatment for the brain give on ND 3, 3A and 4 and the related
part of the brain.

In the paralysis of limbs of one side, the opposite side of the brain gets affected.
Hence, for the treatment of paralysis, treat the opposite side of the brain.

In facial paralysis, treat the same side of the brain.

In flaccid syndromes main treatment should be given on spinal cord.
