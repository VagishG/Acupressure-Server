These autonomic nervous systems control and regulate the following self regulatory
systems :

Temperature control

Wake and sleep mechanism
Fat metabolism

Water metabolism

Genital function (sexual function)
Prespiration

Hunger and feeding mechanism

Hypothalamus is the head ganglion of autonomic nervous system as it participates
in all vital activities of the body relating to sympathetic and parasympathetic
functions.
