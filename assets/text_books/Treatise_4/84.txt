Kidney Meridian (K Meridian)

Location:

In EAV Kidney meridian starts from the root of nail on inner side of V'" toe of foot
whereas in Chinese Acupuncture Kidney meridian starts from K 1 on sole of the foot. After
that location of points from K 1 to K 6 is different in EAV and Chinese Acupuncture. After
K6 the location of all other points are similar as Chinese Acupuncture. In EAV, Chinese
K 1 is described as deep lymph. This meridian goes to the Mambrio-Clavicular junction in
Chest.

Number of Points :

According to EAV there are total 38 points in Kidney meridian whereas in Chinese
Acupuncture total number of points are 27. Dr. R. Voll has discovered new 11 points in
Kidney meridian. Itis a bilateral meridian.
