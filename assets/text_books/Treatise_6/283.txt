Yu Ye (Yuye)
This point lies on the back of the tongue at the
vein to the right of the vinculum linguae.

Hai Quan (Haiquan)
This point of located in the lower side of the
tongue in the centre of the frenum linguae.

Jin Jin (Jinjin)
This point is under the tongue with the tongue
rolled back, at the vein to the left of the
vinculum linguae
