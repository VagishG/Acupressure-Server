A :
In empty condition, numbness comes slowly and it is chronic. In
excess condition, numbness comes suddenly and is acute.
Numbness in ageing comes slowly due to ageing and so there is
deficiency of blood in the related meridian.

Dull sensation like dull soreness or ache indicates that there is
deficiency condition in the related meridian.

Sharp sensation like sharp pains indicates excess condition in
the related meridian.

When there is feeling of warmth along a meridian, then it indicate
increase of heat in the related meridian.

When there is feeling of cold along a meridian, then it indicate
increase of cold in the related meridian.
