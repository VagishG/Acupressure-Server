Yin deficiency

When there is yin deficiency, it causes empty heat. Yin Qi cools. Yin moistens.
When there is yin deficiency, there is dry throat & tongue, There is low grade
fever & five palm heat. Ultimately it causes bleeding through urine. The colour
of blood is bright red. The bleeding is scanty. Prolonged kidney yin deficiency
causes empty fire blazing, which in turn causes blood in urine.
