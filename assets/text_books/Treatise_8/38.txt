It upbears the spleen qi & down bears the stomach qi.
It tonifies the qi of the chest, whichis related to heart and lung.

It also regulates qi and dispels stagnation of the qi in the chest, causing

It down bears the lungs qi, upbears the qi mechanism.

It nourishes original qi and use this point for chronic physical & mental
weakness.

It moves qi in general and is used for stagnation of qi in the middle warmer.
It tonifies lungs qi and strengthens the body after a chronic illness.

It regulates & harmonises the defensive especially when a person has been
attacked by wind and is sweating.
