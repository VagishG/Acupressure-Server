Lymphangitis (cfgesrentet xitez)

Lymphangitis is inflammation of lymph vessels. Local wounds. May cause swelling &
inflammation of lymph nodes. Blood poisoning i.e. sepis spreads along lymph vessels &
causes inflammation of lymph vessel & lymph nodes.
