Taking left arm and left leg as one unit, left palm is 0 and left sole = 9.
Taking both legs as one unit, right sole is 0 and left sole is 9.

Hence. treatment platform for both soles (if affected) is 9 (Left Thumb); for Left
sole - 9 and for Right sole - 0.
