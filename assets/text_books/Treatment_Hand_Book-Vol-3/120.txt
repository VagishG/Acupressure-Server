The Human Brain - Its two basic functional properties :
1) Sensation :
i) | The process by which the central nervous system receives input from the
environment via sensory neurons.
ii) | Bottom up processing.
Perception :
i) The process by which the brain interprets and organizes sensot
information.
ii) Top-down processing.
