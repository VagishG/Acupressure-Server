10. TEN PARTS OF BRAIN / HEAD

(F. No. 9 - 10 Spms)

Generally Tumors / Nodes are near the central line,
Hence the outer central line is '0' (vertical fissure) while
The inner central line is '9' (Horizontal as corpus callosum)

Rest part of the head region is divided on the following two basis :

1.
Right Front Left Left Back Right
A. Basis - Lower Half as 1, 2, 3, 4
2.

Right Front Left Left Back Right
B. Basis - Front Half as 1, 2, 3, 4
